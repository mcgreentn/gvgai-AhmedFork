This is how you control your avatar:
To move as the ShootAvatar, use the arrow keys to turn and move.
Press space to release a bomb_10 (Immovable).

This is how you win:
If Door and ShootAvatar collide, then the Door sprite will be opened.
If the Door sprite is opened, then you Win.

This is how you get points:
If the RandomNPC and the OrientedFlicker collide, then you will gain 5 points.
If the wallBreak (Immovable) and the OrientedFlicker collide, then you will gain 1 points.
If the Door and the ShootAvatar collide, then you will gain 10 points.
