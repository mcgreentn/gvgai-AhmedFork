This is how you control your avatar:
To move as the ShootAvatar, use the arrow keys to turn and move.
Press space to use the sword (OrientedFlicker).

This is how you win:
If nokey (ShootAvatar) and key (Immovable) collide, then the nokey (ShootAvatar) sprite will be transformed into withkey (ShootAvatar).
If Door and withkey (ShootAvatar) collide, then the Door sprite will be opened.
If the Door sprite is opened, then you Win.

This is how you get points:
If the nokey (ShootAvatar) and the key (Immovable) collide, then you will gain 1 points.
If the RandomNPC and the OrientedFlicker collide, then you will gain 2 points.
If the Door and the withkey (ShootAvatar) collide, then you will gain 1 points.
